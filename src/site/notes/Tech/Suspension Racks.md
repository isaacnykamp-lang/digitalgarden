________
*Suspension racks use small amounts of [[Tech/Sun Stone\|Sun Stone]] to tie together physical elements with an electromagnetic lock, usually of handheld devices. The [[Exalted Gauntlets\|Exalted Gauntlets]] as an example, tie down tactical rails to float suspended knuckles in dual positions for forceful or lethal combat.*
