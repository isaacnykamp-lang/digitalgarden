*The 52 Asteroid is comprised entirely of a material dubbed Sun Stone, which rained all over the continent following the cataclysm. It is a miracle material with unique effects and applications. 

*The refined ore generates heat and electromagnetic energy. A melted and supercharged version can be used as a power source for decades, for batteries and fuel cells. A sonically active alloy can devise blades with infinite penetrative power, and cartridges that can repeat fire railed weapons. A laced metal can devise immensely weather resistant coatings and stones that tenaciously resist erosion. Geolocation equipment is powered by microscopic, stable Sun Stone filaments. The charge collecting effects of the stone, the ability to stabilize has rendered nuclear assault moot, as just a few kilos of sunstone can consume and stably release an entire fallout. 

*A major role of seekers is navigating for lightning collection arrays, which leave Sunshard powered hatches in stormy conditions to collect power.

*The most unique application is the [[Tech/Kinetic Sink\|Kinetic Sink]] which has completely changed combat in application. The machine is capable of generating a field which consumes kinetic energy above a threshold. This catch, alongside the total lethality of edged weapons has eliminated the former necessity of armor and reduced the dominance of ranged combat.

*While the ore itself is energy resistant, its supporting components are not. Sunshard attracts lightning due to its strong magnetic field, and large reactive machines are at risk due to this quality.

*It is posited that the [[Eternal Storm\|Eternal Storm]] in the south circles around the crash site of the [[52 Asteroid,\|52 Asteroid,]] leading experts to believe large enough deposits can generate their own weather patterns. It would explain a lot.
