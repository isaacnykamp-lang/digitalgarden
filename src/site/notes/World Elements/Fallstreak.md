________________
Fallstreaks refer to the habitable zone that a large [[Tech/Solace Spire\|Solace Spire]] produces, forming [[storm gutters\|storm gutters]] outside of their electromagnetic field. While the troposphere is peaceful, the vertical range is marked with intense downdrafts and divots that make aerial navigation impossible.
