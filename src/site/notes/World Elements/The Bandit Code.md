_______________
*The code of conduct that most [[Factions/Bandits\|Bandits]] purport to operate by, gentleman's rules for generally peaceful living.*

0. The fund of all payments under the articles is the stock of what is gotten by the expedition, following the same law as other bandits, that is, no prey, no pay.
1. An eye for an eye. Don't target Seekers lest yours be shot.
2. Blood is optional. The best shakedown leaves everyone feeling happy and with their neck intact.
3. Don't mess with Aguids. The wise bandit knows paying for water with blood is a net loss.
4. Nimble fingers and a forgettable face make the best bandit. Get a mask if the latter doesn't work.
5. Honor among thieves applies even if it's shiny. Especially if it's shiny.
6. Don't ask to pull a synth's tail, lots of bandits are synths.
7. The only thing you don't want to filch is other people's business.
8. It's impolite to ask about bionics, and no, you can't steal them.
9. Observe your party's curfew signage.
10. Games of chance cannot be played for money while on assignment.
11. Severe injury will be paid out of the community chest. All unauthorised filchery of the community chest is punishable by marooning or death.
12. If at any time you meet with a prudent woman, the man that offers to meddle with her, without her consent, shall suffer present death.
