_______
Cultivated and domesticated, the [[Timeline/Lost Eras\|Lost Eras]] were unable to drive bees to extinction thanks to their human partnership- all the more important now, with so few places to erect a safe hive. You’ll find their wards out on the roads near cities, selling nutrient dense, spoil-resistant honey to travelers for fair prices. Beeliners are very tight-lipped about their work, and the thrumming hives are usually enough to stave questions from clients.
