______________

Travelers of a seasoned time can find moments of solace in whatever phenomenon causes Echoes, most having entered into a few. Seemingly breaches into a time in the past, usually dating to before the [[Timeline/Lost Eras\|Lost Eras]], Echoes contain mundane settings of their point transfixed in time, and fade away shortly after being observed. In some regard they are real, and the [[Historia\|Historia]] will pay fine rates for any artifacts recovered from the past.
