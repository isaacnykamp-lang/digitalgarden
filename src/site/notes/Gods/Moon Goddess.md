___________________
*The primordial deity and estranged of [[Gods/Sosu\|Sosu]]. Not estranged in ill will, but the two bodies rarely meet. The [[Factions/Tensi/Tensi\|Tensi]] celebrate eclipses as a long lost love.
Due to her faraway nature, her ends are very much her own. But if [[Gods/Sosu\|Sosu]] trusts her without wavering, her intentions must be benevolent. It's no wonder the [[Abyss Children\|Abyss Children]] move most urgently when the nights are moonless.
Her light reflected in the night is the critical measure of their shared philosophy. By some measure, the existence of [[World Elements/Dusk\|Dusk]] is a necessity, but in duress and trial Sosu's light is always watching. Someone needs to stand apart to save the stranded.*
The [[Crescent Scions\|Crescent Scions]] exact her will in physical matters.

*[[Foresight\|Foresight]] is considered the primary trait of her bestowed nomination, the sigil of [[Sigils/Manteis\|Manteis]].

*She is also responsible for the [[Hyades.\|Hyades.]]
