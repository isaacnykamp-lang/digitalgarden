__________
*The executive hand of the [[Factions/Ansolari\|Ansolari]]. The highest of the erudite don’t concern themselves with enforcing their might, but entrust its occupation to their lineage of knights. Selected and bred exclusively for their martial quality, trained from birth to be peerless warriors, any augmentation required. None of the order would ever think of betraying their purpose, ensured all the more by their biomechanical enhancements and striking features- the Praetor are impossible to miss. Identity is earned through achievement. Prayers and harvesting of [[Tech/Sun Stone\|Sun Stone]] channel power to a project of devotion known only by the greatest of ranks.
__________

*[[Centurion\|Centurion]]
   Nobody at all but the tool of a higher will. The centurions are dedicated to the security of the Ansolari’s dominion, and will follow orders without question. Tall, masked and shrouded in robes of white, swords the size of a grown man and the pinnacle of human achievement.
__________
*[[DnD Enemies/Basic Foes/(CR 1) Noble\|(CR 1) Noble]]
   One of a unique hundred, the commanders and overseers reporting to the Consuls. They sport bionic enhancements concealed under their robes, extending their might beyond human limits. While granted a greater voice than the centurion, their names are in title only and their actions are beholden to the will of the Consul. The Aethis may be impelled to act as inquisitor to root out rebellion and dissent.

____________

*[[Consul\|Consul]]
   Each of three consuls is loyal, yoked to a branch of the Ansolari order and tasked with invoking their interests. These generals as a whole act on the behalf of the Praetor, and hold immense power in the order- as such, their own designs move independent of the [[High Ones\|High Ones]]. As the greatest of executors, the Consuls are alone privated to show their face. 

   *The [[High Ones\|High Ones]] must gain the allegiance of all three consuls to rule rightly, or be a crippled leader. 
   Two sitting Consuls have consorted with [[Factions/Anassa\|Anassa]] and the Basilisks respectively, the corruption of light’s order only waiting for a signal to tear asunder.

__________________________

*[[DnD Enemies/Praetor/(CR 8) De-gaussed Exul\|(CR 8) De-gaussed Exul]]
   *Whether vagrant, defective, infidels or insubordinate, there are exiles to the order. The formal body of Exul defected upon the [[Schism of Augmentation\|Schism of Augmentation]], maintaining that bionic platement was an inroad to a Basilisk scheme, growing even beneath the feet of the Consuls. 

   Most outcasts are hunted and killed in their locales, maintaining that the former order has been subverted beyond saving. While others grew a conscience and refused to be a tool any longer. 

   At any rate, the Exul in the waking world faces hardship on account of their orders’ persecution, and equal avoidance due to their stature.
