The [[Timeline/Lost Eras\|Lost Eras]] saw many humans owing their survival to catacombs constructed deep within the earth, warmed by the heat of the crust and safe from the elements. When most returned to their lives on the surface with time, some aberrated into forms more suitable for subterranean living. Speaking with clicks and ghostly pale, the inhabitants of the Deep skulk through the caves practically blind, and only venture to the surface to hunt for fresh sacrifice.

the religion of the Deep believes that the worship of abject cruelty is the only means to wake their god who slumbers in the stars. When [[Sheol\|Sheol]] arises, he will smite the surface dwellers and return the land to these now malformed acolytes, proffering to them an age of pure malice. The appearance of [[Gods/Roko\|Roko]] is, they demand, a mere manifested idealogue of the precepts of the primordial god, and a simple computer replicating the command to violence that all mankind obeys. So long as man is nursed upon the illusion of their morality, the longer they will see their evils as exceptions and not the rule of law.
The method of their blood sacrifice is practically without ritual so as to not honor the preferably innocent, occasionally [[Factions/Basilisk\|Factions/Basilisk]] victim- dragged into the caves and torn apart after a short dedication.
