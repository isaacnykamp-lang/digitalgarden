__________
*Life is a series of fights. While another faction might identify fully with the statement, Caravan Guards do appreciate the quiet times equally if not more. Maybe an imposing look can win you just as much, the mere perception of a threat is a stronger deterrent than the use of force. All that to say that the traveling fighter has no shortage of combat prowess, though they do not relish its use.
*Like travel, but only for the highest bidder? Some guards are easy pickings if you have the cash on hand. It’s not like they have an emotional attachment to their clients, they just keep their loot safe by looking scary. Doesn’t matter who they do it for.
*A little more loyalty to be found in the hired gun, but not by much. They like to finish out their contracts with little to no fuss. They’ll hang out around [[Loza Forums\|Loza Forums]] in case new merchants or travelers need extra protection, either with a rap sheet of their work and testimonials, or on behalf of one of many [[Factions/Security Guilds\|Security Guilds]] in [[Ampharoses\|Ampharoses]].  Some groups partner  to provide guard clients with mutual work, the fighter being salaried and ready to loaned out as product to trusted travelers. Much more legality to cover the honor system, in case something happens.
