______________________
Security Guilds ply the trades of [[Factions/Mercenaries\|Mercenaries]] on a greater level of responsibility and contractual obligation, operating as both the bulk body that discourages bandits from sacking small settlements and keeping organised caravans safe in transit. They wield a stronger influence in such places, but as they're deeply peer-to-peer competitive, find it a hard ask to annex a city themselves and force its denizens to pay an extortionate price with another guild ever on their heels. Pay requires the trust of their clients, and even bandits need shelter from the valleys from time to time. An amenable solution can always be arranged. 
Obviously as such, the aims and promises vary wildly between guilds, but the most prominent have stations in most major cities to better handle their human assets. The Fowlfoxes, Guild 52, North Party and the BVU are generally the most common, operating on liability contracts with their hired assets.
