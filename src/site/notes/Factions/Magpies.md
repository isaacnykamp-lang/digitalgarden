Run by [[DnD Enemies/Vulture Teeth/(CR 15) Kukura Krahé\|(CR 15) Kukura Krahé]], counter-banditry that predates on the territory of [[Factions/Vulture Teeth\|Vulture Teeth]]. True to the name, they're after their share of riches and will occasionally play nice with government operations, but their loyalty is truly beholden to anything shiny. Their code of conduct sets them apart from Vulture Teeth in that they refuse to trade in human lives, and will viciously undermine their larger counterpart at any chance.
