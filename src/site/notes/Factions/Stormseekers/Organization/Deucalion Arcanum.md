______________________
*The primary academy dedicated to training students of the finer arts of the [[Factions/Stormseekers/Seekers\|Seekers]], an enormous campus directly adjacent to the [[Atrium Archive.\|Atrium Archive.]] Members of the academy gain progressively unlimited access to one of the largest remaining libraries on [[Dimensional Locations/Arta\|Arta]].
Measurement of incoming students is strictly monitored by the Deucalion so as to not oversaturate roles.

*Young students in the Arcanum are split into two primary tracks- 
>The Autogenes ([[House Atlas\|House Atlas]])
>Those in education for Lighthousing, Thueltography and in-Guild operations pay an unsupported tuition for course tracks and wear the typical blue shades of the organization. 
>
>The Exogenes ([[House Hyades\|House Hyades]])
>Students on an accelerated track with two segments; a supported tuition for those pursuing a completed [[Band Test\|Band Test]], and a tuition-free equipment, advanced methodology and ethos track for those who have completed their field training. 
>The Exogenes are given preferential treatment in most cases, but have a very demanding course list. They are viewed with the same regard as upper-classmen, and wear white robes during their stint at the academy. 
>Students who have completed the band test outside of the jurisdiction of the direct Guild exam take an extra class to validate their band, but are eligible for a [[Proctor Ring\|Proctor Ring]] before other students.

The Guild has a strict code of conduct that begins at the Deucalion that all future practitioners must follow. Additionally, each house has a headmaster who elect and report to the [[Nepheles\|Nepheles]], administration with the charge of all goings on of the school and archives. Together, the five headmasters make up the [[Taurus\|Taurus]]. Among their standard management of wings, they also interview incoming students and determine eligible tuition agreements.

>-The [[Aesyle Wing\|Aesyle Wing]], house of knowledge for lighthouse keepers, sun stone acquisition and supplements of the [[Historia\|Historia]] answers to Headmaster Asyle.
>-The [[Coronis Wing\|Coronis Wing]], house of knowledge for thueltography and data collectors answers to Headmaster Coronis
>-The [[Cleis Wing\|Cleis Wing]], house of knowledge for operations and management of the Guild proper answers to Headmaster Cleis
>-The [[Aethra Wing\|Aethra Wing]], house of knowledge for practical application of thueltography and Stormseeking in the field answers to Headmaster Aethra.
>The [[Hyas Wing\|Hyas Wing]], house of knowledge of advanced field practices answers to Headmaster Hyas. 

The Deucalion has three seats in [[Factions/Stormseekers/Organization/The Parliament\|The Parliament]]. Controversially, Aethra and Hyas are not represented in any seating to the Guild proper despite being critically supported by the Deucalion.
