*Storm Charters, a sub-sect of [[Factions/Stormseekers/Seekers\|Seekers]], provide the bulk of research and curricular resources for the Guild. Charged with collecting data to keep general forecasts and models up to date, they spend more time in the plains at stationary posts to observe weather patterns. Not as practiced as Seekers, they rarely travel alone.
