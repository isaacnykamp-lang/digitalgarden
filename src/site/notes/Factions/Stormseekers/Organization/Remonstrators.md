*Some custodians of the [[Factions/Stormseekers/Seekers\|Seekers]] are specially trained to deliver messages across long distances on behalf of the Guild, of variable rank and class. To effectively enforce their expectations, remonstrators also have authority to apprehend Seekers with bounties to their name or those branded as [[Neque Conscia\|Neque Conscia]], those who do not reflect the Guild’s standards.
