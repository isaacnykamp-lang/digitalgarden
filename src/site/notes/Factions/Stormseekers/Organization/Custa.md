*The custodians of the [[Factions/Stormseekers/Seekers\|Seekers]] oversee its day to day hub functions. Mundane administrative duties as well as culling the client pool in the [[Loza Forums\|Loza Forums]], the workers of Guild Hubs maintain the spotless reputation that the Guild comports itself by. The data collections, [[Artifact Halls\|Artifact Halls]] and schoolhouses of the Guild are vast, and owe their application to the Custa.
