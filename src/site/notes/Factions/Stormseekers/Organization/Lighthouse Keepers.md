*Imagine the Field Seeker as an even greater nerd. Lighthouse Keepers are more hermit-like, specialized in their knowledge of [[Tech/Sun Stone\|Sun Stone]] and its procurement. They are essential and lucrative assets to the Guild, spending most of their time researching and maintaining the [[Solace Spires\|Solace Spires]] that all cities have. They occasionally leave their solitude to oversee collection of [[Tech/Sun Stone\|Sun Stone]] deposits.
