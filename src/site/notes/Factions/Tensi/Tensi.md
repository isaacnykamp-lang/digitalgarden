___________
*The Tensi are immigrants of [[Regions/The Dusk Line\|The Dusk Line]], their lands swallowed in the eternal storm long ago. Formerly mild fishermen off the coast, they gather in exclusivity under bellies of towns to worship their sun deity, [[Gods/Sosu\|Sosu]]. They are well acquainted with the tribulations and cycles of life by their mythos, and as such, practically relish hardship so long as reprieve can be found in the light of the sun and congregations of the faithful. Their warrior queen of the sun is an imperfect but loving being. Her light moderated by the red caps they wear to stave off burned scalps. Their peace among tribulation is enviable- their hardihood equally venerated, a kind people thoroughly unafraid of a fight.
