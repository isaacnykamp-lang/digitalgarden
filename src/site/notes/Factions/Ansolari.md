The Ansolari are led by the [[Characters/Consul Magnus\|Consul Magnus]] of the time and command the [[Factions/Solari Praetor\|Factions/Solari Praetor]], the functional heads of Solace and passive dictators. Generally speaking, they leave well enough alone, providing wonders of reverse engineered technology to their populace in exchange for energy and ideological guidance. Their lower levels are entrenched in bureaucratic nonsense, while the Praetor and appointed cardinals are free to act how they will. While some might worship their supposed spotless splendor, most have heard their promises of a second space age for long enough that it feels a hollow promise.
