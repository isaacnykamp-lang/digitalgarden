_________
*An autonomous colony skirts some plains that call themselves Vulture Teeth, which makes no sense as birds don’t have teeth. The most scraggly of the bunch, they waylay travelers with numbers and are a general nuisance. They're especially ragged for their willingness to traffic slaves, and lug much of their ill-gotten gain to a mythical figure named [[The Big Bird\|The Big Bird]].
