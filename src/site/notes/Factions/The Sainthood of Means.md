_______________________
Called Meanings, the dominant organized religion in [[Dimensional Locations/Arta\|Arta]] acknowledges but demonizes the existence of all gods, affirming the moderation of which only humans are capable. The function of the saints is to placate populaces against their stronger desires, preaching asceticism in lieu of the 'barbaric and cruel-' a useful tool for fledgling governments for dulling the senses of the masses. Though the tempest of the outside world sheds their worldview some merit, 
the reality is that the Saints rarely promote any kind of abstemiousness and in their quietude, affirm the timid and fearful into a worldview that is fundamentally adversarial to human experience; seeing the plains as a danger, the outsider as unpredictable, and the gods as vindictive.

They claim the [[First Seeker\|First Seeker]] as a patron saint of virtuous means, though this distances them from the [[Factions/Stormseekers/Seeker's Guild\|Seeker's Guild]]. 
Though they don't directly encourage it, the Meanings are a persecuting force against the [[Factions/Tensi/Tensi\|Tensi]].
