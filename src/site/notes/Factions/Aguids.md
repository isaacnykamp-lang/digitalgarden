*Known usually for their tolerable nature, the Aguid faction spends their travel carting and purifying vast water supplies for a chance to pinch a penny off thirsty travelers. But there’s a reason [[Factions/Bandits\|bandits]] don’t dare to bother them, and it’s certainly not out of altruism. Don’t get on their bad side.
