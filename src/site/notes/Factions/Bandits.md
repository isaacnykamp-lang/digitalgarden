_________
*Bandits are doubtless a plague in any setting, pack hunting for personal gain and thieving whenever possible. There’s probably some lack of actualization in that lifestyle. However, you’ll find on the plains that there’s a certain code of conduct in their parties- most caravans have the capacity to hurt them as well, so something of a gentleman’s truce for some coin can always be arranged. Their allegiance will likely always be with themselves.
*A certain kind of pilferer pantomimes as a stranded traveler to hitchhike with caravans, only to make off with their treasures at night and repeat the process until they reach an Eastern city. As for those urban centers, you certainly know the kind of waif, pickpocket and snatcher you’re bound to find. The best make it a practical profession.
