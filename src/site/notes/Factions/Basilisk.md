*Immensely secretive, one cannot truly know the ends of this ancient cult, only that their ranks are not populated by outsiders and they have no need to recruit. Their aim is to find something called the Can. Based on the relatively common knowledge of heptacomputers having long been buried in the ground, it may have something to do with these enormous submerged obelisks and their disappearance. The only evidence of their existence are the [[Snakestones\|Snakestones]] that were carved into select waystones at the displeasure of the Guild in the distant past.
