____________
*Referential to the Polynesians whose original homes have been swallowed by the rising tides. Most of the mainland Hawaiki were forced off of the Hawaiian islands before the [[Timeline/Departure\|Departure]].
They live in hopes that a sailor as intrepid as Hawai'iloa may be raised among them to find their homes, and if the sea refuses to loose them, a warrior brave enough to beat it back.

Knowing of the fates of the [[Factions/Tensi/Tensi\|Tensi]], the Hawaiki have always been staunch supporters of their fellows. Knowing there is not a home to return to but for mere hope feels all too similar. The outsiders should do what they can for each other.
