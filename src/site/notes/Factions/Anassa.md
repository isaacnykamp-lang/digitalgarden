_________________
*A more organized form of urban thievery, Anassa are prolific black market dealers responsible for major epidemics of hard drugs in the Southwest. They’ve become so eponymous with shady deals that they’ve caught the Praetor’s ire, only weaseling away through their under-table dealings with [[Locations/Solace\|Solace]]’s bureaucracy.
They're led by a vicious man named [[Quyen.\|Quyen.]]
