The Lost Eras refer to a point in [[Timeline/History\|History]] spanning a debated range of 600 years prior to modern settlement, when civilization was rendered impossible by the acts of [[Coalitions\|Coalitions]], namely the [[Solari\|Solari]]. The construction of the [[Locations/The Shipyards\|The Shipyards]], massive heptacomputer farms that belched water vapor, and low-grade global emissions from industrial production began a runaway cascade that came to full force long after the [[Solari\|Solari]] had left for [[Mars\|Mars]], exactly their intent. Arguably, this shift towards a gambled solution was catalyzed by the [[52 Asteroid\|52 Asteroid]].
What followed were [[Ash Storms\|Ash Storms]] from destabilized tectonic plates (especially the Yellowstone Caldera reducing itself to [[Regions/The Blast Zone\|The Blast Zone]]), continued mass famine from sunless winters, mass animal extinction, ocean acidity, [[Hypercanes\|Hypercanes]], [[Carrington Events\|Carrington Events]], [[Kessler Syndrome Cascades\|Kessler Syndrome Cascades]], scorched summers and coastal flooding, a net rise of 70 feet of ocean within the time frame- 
All of which rendered humans themselves on the brink of extinction, and in the process, ridding them of all protective and predictive infrastructure. Hence, the Lost Eras, as most surviving documentation as to former history was lost if not preserved by a lineage. What could be considered as fact before the occurrence of this epoch is hotly contested, with the leading scholars championing the subject, the [[Historia\|Historia]], relying heavily on archeological findings.
One of the most knowledgeable on the subject, the [[Interlopers/The Sand Witch\|The Sand Witch]], is incredibly selective with whom she reveals the dark past to. 
Many projects like [[Characters/AXIS-01\|AXIS-01]] and the [[Resonance Project\|Resonance Project]], the [[Locations/The Caches\|The Caches]], the [[Locations/Seed Vaults\|Seed Vaults]], the [[Locations/Orbital Outpost\|Orbital Outpost]] are remnants of Solari projets that have been abandoned during the epoch.

With the eventual reclamation of [[Tech/Sun Stone\|Sun Stone]] from remaining dormant tech and chips scrounged from [[Starfalls\|Starfalls]], [[Tech/Solace Spire\|Solace Spire]]s were constructed to keep the deadly storms at bay. Since then, a period of relative ecological stability has allowed for the reintroduction of civil life- though most people live without knowing what was taken from them, especially with the [[Factions/Ansolari\|Ansolari]] spreading propaganda about former events. They might just believe it themselves.

The [[Factions/Stormseekers/Seeker's Guild\|Seeker's Guild]] recounts this era with as much detail as possible in their annals and woodcuts the conduct of their mission with the legendary first [[Factions/Stormseekers/Seekers\|Seekers]], namely the [[Interlopers/Storm Shepherd\|Storm Shepherd]]. 


The Lost Eras are responsible for the underground projects of the [[Factions/Basilisk\|Factions/Basilisk]]s, the creation of the [[Factions/Stormseekers/Seekers\|Seekers]] and the [[Factions/Stormseekers/Seeker's Guild\|Seeker's Guild]], the displacement of the [[Factions/Tensi/Tensi\|Tensi]] and [[Factions/Hawaiki\|Hawaiki]], the advanced positions of the [[Factions/Ansolari\|Ansolari]] in [[Locations/Solace\|Solace]], the underground cities of [[Locations/Bywater\|Bywater]] and [[Locations/Gila Canyon\|Gila Canyon]], The [[Locations/Grottoes\|Grottoes]], and the creation of the [[Second Covenant\|Second Covenant]].
