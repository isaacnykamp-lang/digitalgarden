_____________
A [[Factions/Tensi/Tensi\|Tensi]] holiday, celebrated on the first eclipse major of the year and with minor celebrations thereafter. Told to be the reunion of the celestial bodies in dedication, and recounted with ritual storytelling, lights deep into the evening, and solar-themed outfits.
