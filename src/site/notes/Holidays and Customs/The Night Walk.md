__________
Primarily a [[Factions/Tensi/Tensi\|Tensi]] festival, though the [[Factions/Hawaiki\|Hawaiki]] and [[Factions/Disciples\|Disciples]] are regular participants to some degree. Festival commemorates the passed using the representation of empty chairs, participants reenact the march of the dead alongside handheld food, parades of lights, fireworks, and paint. Participants walk a route until the fall asleep in the wee hours, and those who successfully parade the entire night sleep the following day.
