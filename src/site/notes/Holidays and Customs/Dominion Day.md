____________________
The [[Factions/Tensi/Tensi\|Tensi]] call the summer solstice Dominion Day on account of the sun's maximised presence. Either burn the excess of crops and worn down junk as offering, or take a swim in the dead of night if there's nearby habitable water for luck.
