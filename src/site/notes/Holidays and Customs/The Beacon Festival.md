The ultimate goal of the [[Factions/Ansolari\|Ansolari]] is to show to their gods their remaining presence through the very atmosphere. Surely their eye attuned to their old earth from Mars would spot their parade of lights and overclock from their enormous [[Tech/Solace Spire\|Solace Spire]], all the energy they saved over the year to make their city a beacon visible from space. If the [[Solari\|Solari]] see their call, surely they will come home and deliver their children to their paradise...
