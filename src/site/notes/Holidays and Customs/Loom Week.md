________________

The [[Factions/Disciples\|Disciples]] and occasional [[Factions/Nomads\|Nomads]] will meet en masse to collect and share their wares over a week of mercantile festivities, sharing music, culture, thrifted and inscribed artifacts, and more in a swath of tents. Disciple children are ritually given fiat money to barter candies and tokens while the adults compete to design stunning clothing and innovate the new year's vogue.
