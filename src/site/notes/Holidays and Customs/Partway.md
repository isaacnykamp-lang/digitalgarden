*It's befitting of [[Factions/Stormseekers/Seekers\|Seekers]] to offer small caravans a farewell gift upon completing their journey. Most often they will offer a round of drinks as a measure of goodwill, and the custom can be seen as a litmus test of bond between parties; most understand that as an obligation it's a small token. For the more endeared, a Partway might consist of jewelry or items of greater affection. They serve the dual purpose of affection, and ensuring that a caravan might ask the Guild specifically for your future services.
