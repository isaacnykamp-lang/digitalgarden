________________
The common calendar of [[Dimensional Locations/Arta\|Arta]] is synodic, thus drifts seasons by 11 days or so. The remaining days allocated to reset the lunar cycle properly land on the back of the calendar, and build into  New Year celebrations with festivities occurring on the first and last of the over-week. Generally a time to have extra time without judgment at the shortest time of solstice.
