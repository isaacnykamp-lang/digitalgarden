___________________
Legend of a bifurcated story, whether referring to the demi-being or historical figure; though both are conflated into mythology. Seekers consider this wanderer as their founding autarch who taught their methods from a tribe into a guild, the first Seeker of the Lost Eras. Their galleries are filled with recovered figments that symbolize their origins. Whereas the Meanings revere the figure as an ascended pillar of virtue and ascribe a constant, godlike status corroborated by a few crazed wreck survivors.
