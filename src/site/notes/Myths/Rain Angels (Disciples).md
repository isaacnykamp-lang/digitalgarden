The [[Factions/Disciples\|Disciples]] do have an exception to their encouraging patterns towards storms. One legend tells of the Blue Party, of which the colour is prohibited as a primary insofar as superstition goes. All killed in a storm too powerful in the distant past, they serve in memoriam to warn those of the conditions to stay away. Most often seen by children before or during cataclysmic events.
