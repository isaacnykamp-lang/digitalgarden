______________
An old tale intended to instill adaptability in [[Factions/Disciples\|Disciples]]. Young listeners would hear tell of the shapeshifting spirit of Jugara, a synth whose powers enabled them to solve the problems of their neighbours. Conveniently spinning endless tales of their travels, Jugara provided elders with a 'see the problem, solve the problem' moral message.
