_________
*Midia* is the easternmost city in the primary west of [[Dimensional Locations/Arta\|Arta]]. 

As a small agricultural site, Midia is largely self-sufficient in the manner that most plains towns are- autonomous regions that cooperate with larger governments as city states, locally operating their exports and securities with standard elected mayors and private austere interests.
