______________
*One of the oldest cities, it has never required its citizens to have an ID card. Likely due to the industry it provided, with enormous forges built to mold megastructure components like heptacomputers and spacecraft. Now that the need for such workmanship has left, this city built into the cliffs of the sea sustains itself on local fishery and seedy vagrants. It’s the place to go for people with nowhere else to go.

*While the East side is impressively wealthy, most of the town is dangerous and best avoided. 
Most trade is sustained by fishing off the isthmus between [[Valley Lake\|Valley Lake]] and [[The Pacific Ocean\|The Pacific Ocean]], either by the drop-off of the continental shelf, or the [[Sea Trunks\|Sea Trunks]].
Local industry is not entirely dead, as its proximity to [[Locations/Solace\|Solace]] makes it well-positioned to supply raw material conversion. Among these organisations are [[Companies/Shimuzu Pharmaceuticals\|Shimuzu Pharmaceuticals]] and [[Factions/Triple A's Titles\|Triple A's Titles]].

*Bywater is inhabited by a large inset population of Koryo-Saram, thus swaths of the city are covered in Korean or Russian (in universe equivalent) signage.
