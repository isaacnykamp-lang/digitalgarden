*North of the plains in [[Regions/The Badlands\|The Badlands]]. Passing west leads one to [[Regions/The Blast Zone\|The Blast Zone]].  Relatively lawless and rugged. Although most travelers from [[Locations/Midia\|Midia]] leave for Badstone, they don't accept most newcomers.
