*A sizable and aged commonwealth of cities located in the higher altitudes of the northwest Sierra Nevada. The city rises above pristine groves of old growth redwoods. The tradeoff for clear skies presents itself in bitter cold for much of the year.
The home of the [[Factions/Ansolari\|Ansolari]], their [[Factions/Solari Praetor\|Factions/Solari Praetor]], and the largest body of [[Factions/Tensi/Tensi\|Tensi]] immigrants, and [[Factions/Anassa\|Anassa]] primary operations. This area's [[Tech/Solace Spire\|Solace Spire]] is drip-fed energy to cycle back to the [[August Research Division\|August Research Division]], and all citizens in the region are currently required to donate a daily ration of battery power.

The Ansolari are interested in the idealistic end of Solace, and leave much of the actual leg work to the [[Production Magnate\|Production Magnate]]. Headed by [[Characters/Silver, Foreman of Trade\|Silver, Foreman of Trade]], commerce travels through his tangled web of ingrained interests. Both butt heads more than they agree these days, as the Praetor are resorted to forceful measures to receive their energy tithes. They completely went over the foreman's head on their Spire expansion project. Whether he's helping to bankroll Anassa's anarchist movement as retaliation is all but spoken.


*The Colonist [[Solari\|Solari]] left for Mars, and their exiles have taken root in the city, waiting for their ancestors to return. There’s a certain obsession with progress and making the Solari proud, but a walk down any street in Solace will show you profound willful ignorance. Old factories, launch pads and cold, empty streets littered with poverty amongst long-dormant technological wonders.
