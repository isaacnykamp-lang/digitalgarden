*The southern trail away from [[Locations/Pharus\|Pharus]], marked by a jet-black stone rising out of the desert and marking entry into the [[Locations/Yavapai Desert\|Yavapai Desert]].*
Stepping foot onto the mountain itself is forbidden- the old tales say an entire tribe of ghosts rest undisturbed at its peak.
