___________
*Pharus is a medium sized township in the [[Central Mountains\|Central Mountains]], known best for its bustling trades and lavish headquarters of the [[Factions/Stormseekers/Seeker's Guild\|Seeker's Guild]]. Most of its local economy revolves around trades that support their role, a primary importer of the [[Factions/Disciples\|Disciples]] dyes and watertight fabrics, a booming town for vehicle fabrication and sensitive equipment.

Pharus contains [[House Castellanus\|House Castellanus]], [[House Opacus\|House Opacus]] and [[House Spissatus\|House Spissatus]], all major donors of the [[Factions/Stormseekers/Seeker's Guild\|Seeker's Guild]]. 
[[House Castellanus\|House Castellanus]] fabricate weather resistant [[ESQA\|ESQA]] building material and the foundations of many Guild structures. They provide security equipment, design labor intensive industry material, and have two representatives in the [[Guild Parliament\|Guild Parliament]].
[[House Opacus\|House Opacus]] design sensitive material and forecasting equipment on behalf of the [[Urbus Council\|Urbus Council]], who oversee the intent and accuracy of products pertaining to their work. The [[Urbus Observatorium\|Urbus Observatorium]] are the world's premier atmospheric researchers and require custom made equipment constantly, shipping much of it north to their remote research facilities. [[House Opacus\|House Opacus]] has three seats in the [[Guild Parliament\|Guild Parliament]].

[[House Spissatus\|House Spissatus]] design and specialise in textiles, tailoring equipment for the Guild as well as travellers. Most gear you'll see in the flatlands will have their hand somewhere in the design process, though it is often inferior to the [[Factions/Disciples\|Disciples]] tailoring. [[House Spissatus\|House Spissatus]] has two seats in the [[Guild Parliament\|Guild Parliament]].

Between these three houses, Pharus is designed for intake and outflow of traveling bands and [[Factions/Stormseekers/Seekers\|Seekers]] at leave, full of lavish temporary housing, [[Ampharoses\|Ampharoses]] for hauling freight and ample entertainment for carousing at. Full of Seeker celebrity, gossip and collections of niche interests. 

It could all make one forget that while this is their headquarters, the city itself is locally governed. The soft power here is no secret, however, especially with [[House Spissatus\|House Spissatus]] webbed in shady dealings with the local government, black market *and* [[Locations/Solace\|Solace]].

Pharus has been caught in a cold war with [[Locations/Solace\|Solace]] due to the situation in [[Locations/Midia\|Midia]] being a clear and hostile annexation by proxy parties. While [[House Opacus\|House Opacus]] is in favor of a denouncement of the [[Factions/Ansolari\|Ansolari]], [[House Spissatus\|House Spissatus]] is too enmeshed with them to budge, while [[House Castellanus\|House Castellanus]] is undecided- While Pharus provides a critical trade route, they are heavily reliant on [[Locations/Solace\|Solace]]'s agriculture. Denouncement would make plain [[Factions/Stormseekers/Seekers\|Seekers]] as a non-neutral entity, and [[Judge Abbas\|Judge Abbas]] is especially dedicated to keeping the Houses out of the Guild's politics.
