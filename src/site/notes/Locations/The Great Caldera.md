*North of [[Regions/The Basin\|The Basin]] and west of the [[Central Mountains\|Central Mountains]], the former Yellowstone Caldera jettisons basalt magma into smoldering pools. Technically dormant, its eruption was one of the major primers behind the apocalyptic elements of  the [[Timeline/Lost Eras\|Lost Eras]].
