***

*A recreational synthetic drug (tysertraline quad-methylprednisolone) synthesized from pharmaceutical painkillers and household/industrial chemicals. Epidemic usage in places like [[Locations/Bywater\|Bywater]], [[Sika\|Sika]], [[Frolin\|Frolin]], and [[Locations/Midia\|Midia]]. 

*The purity of the drug varies based on the provider, contaminants and mixing quality bring out increasingly adverse side effects. Dirty or diluted strains increase psychoactive properties and damage blood capillaries. Hence the name Scabby, addicts develop blackened and scaly skin, with severe cases developing necrosis and widespread tissue damage centering around points of insertion. 

*Administered by needle or ingestion.

*The effects are similar to opioids, with feelings of intense relaxation and euphoria, often coupled with mild, pleasant hallucinations. Users may describe floating, disembodied music, or all sensation being amplified and pleasing. Music or a sense of rhythmic motion is a hallmark of Scabby variants, addicts can be identified by their incessant humming when on a high. The drug fries dopamine receptors with extended use. Addicts, due to the serotonin activity of the SSRI mimic the base ingredients affect, will experience excruciating comedowns (skipping between deleterious phases of tonic immobility, scratchy breath and head pain, to overstimulated aggression, paranoia, and extreme hyperalgesia, that is, pain sensitivity) and require the drug to maintain simple baseline.

Well-to-do citizens may microdose pure strains labeled with the nicknames of tunes, mellow, or thrum– being properly synthesized dampens the effects and negates comedowns, producing a strong relaxant with mild hallucinations. This form reduces anxiety and ‘boosts productivity,’ but is not without risk. The common consensus among some that mellow is a safe recreational drug inadvertently ruins lives.
