________
*High pressure variance feeds the mountain snows west of [[Regions/The Basin\|The Basin]], and strengthens the wind patterns surrounding the mountains. The hot anabatic Diablo winds form zones of unbearably heated and gusty semi-arid valleys. While this means less storms for longer periods, thus trees and shrubbery, these mountain shadows are where the strongest storms begin to cook.
