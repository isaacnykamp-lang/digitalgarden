________________
*East of the [[Central Mountains\|Central Mountains]] are flat and flooded grasslands, battered by unrelenting winds and a makeshift coast. While the storm lane has shifted into a gutter in the southwest, the Plains are still the most tempestuous storm conditions on earth for tornadoes and hurricanes alike. 
The Plains are characterised by the most eclectic populations of the poor, typically homesteads fulfilling their roles out of the family without much formal trade. They produce impressive rates of their primary export, accelerated crops and livestock, as the regions resources are largely uncontested- many [[Factions/Stormseekers/Seekers\|Seekers]] spend a chunk of their training in the Plains observing the fickle weather with the safety of well-traveled locals.
The density of [[Natural Phenomena/Echoes\|Echoes]] is at its highest in this region.
