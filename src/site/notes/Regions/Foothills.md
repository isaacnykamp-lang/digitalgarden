*On the landward side of the [[Cascades\|Cascades]], [[Central Mountains\|Central Mountains]] and [[Sierra Nevada\|Sierra Nevada]], one can find pleasant slopes with lush vegetation and straggled trees. These areas usually have mild and durable seasons, only inhospitable for unpredictable swings. Old [[Factions/Stormseekers/Seekers\|Seekers]] routes are marked by weather resistant stones, leading caravans down paths of brief respite. Communes tend to spend long stays in this habitable zone– something akin to mobile villages. You'll usually find [[Locations/The Sunflower\|The Sunflower]], [[Factions/Vulture Teeth\|Vulture Teeth]], [[Locations/Harvesting Stations\|Harvesting Stations]] and [[Shepherds\|Shepherds]] in this median area, plying their trades where they can quickly retreat back into the mountains.
