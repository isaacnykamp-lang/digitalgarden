*Named for a distinctive ring of scales on its head, these snakes are opportunistic eaters and will emerge from burrows to hunt for eggs or small mammals. They are general pests that the [[Creatures/Tuft Swallow, Tuft Squirrel\|Tuft Swallow, Tuft Squirrel]] guard each others young against.
