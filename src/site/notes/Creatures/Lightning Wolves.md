_______
These bluish, spotted lean dogs have almost whip-like tails. Hunting in packs of three at most, they trade the endurance of most canines for speed and surprise, running down their mammalian prey much like cheetahs. Their habitable zone is quite small, on account of their general ill-suitedness for weather events. Most often found in the habitable zones of [[Regions/The Diablos\|The Diablos]].
