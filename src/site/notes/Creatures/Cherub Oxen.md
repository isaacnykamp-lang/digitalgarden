_____
*A name given on behalf of their halo-like horns and exceptionally gentle demeanor. This breed of hardy bovine was originally of African origin, but acclimatized well to the lengthy travel and plentiful foraging space. They are a favorite for [[Shepherds\|Shepherds]] with heavier convoys.
