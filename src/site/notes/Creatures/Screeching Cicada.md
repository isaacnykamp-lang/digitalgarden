__________
*They’re more often called screechers. You’ll often hear them before seeing them, stridulating in the dirt somewhere. But enough adverse conditions and they may start swarming, to the dismay of farmers' crops and their ears. For most, they can be a nuisance at best. A fun game for children to hunt down alongside [[grasshoppers\|grasshoppers]] in the [[Regions/Great Plains\|Great Plains]].
