______
*Found in the more populated edges of basins, these foxes emerge after rains to hunt the sudden appearance of waterfowl that predate new flood zones. They are a muted blue color, and excellent swimmers. Not to be confused with the [[Fowlfoxes\|Fowlfoxes]], a securities guild operating out of [[Locations/Pharus\|Pharus]].
