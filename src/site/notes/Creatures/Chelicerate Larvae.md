*The young of the greater chelicerate appear as segmented mites. Armored with shell plating, they scrounge in the [[Locations/Grottoes\|Grottoes]] after smaller cave fare, in and out of shallow pools with pincer-like mouths. Most Chelicerates will never grow larger than knee height, bumbling around in their plates without hostility. However, some grow to the size of [[Alpha Chelicerates\|Alpha Chelicerates]] and develop a severely aggressive countenance.
