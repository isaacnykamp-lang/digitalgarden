______
*A peculiar breed of gull-like raptor devoid of the latter’s definitive traits- namely, intelligence. These pack hunters are drawn to motion on the wide open plains. If prey are spotted, they attack in earnest, flinging themselves at much larger prey with their armored heads. Their speared beaks can take down a chance helpless mammal for them to eat, but their headfirst strategy has resulted in a respectively dumb bird. If you can bait them into a dive, a whole flock can make a suitable chicken dinner for weeks.
