_________
*Unique creatures in that they have adapted to such a size that storms are often of no concern to them. Their leathery outer pelts are both hardy and non-conductive, and they possess enormous horns to entrench themselves in place. Even the claws of a [[Creatures/Haast's Eagle\|Creatures/Haast's Eagle]] can’t penetrate their thick hide, and only channeling weapons suit to pierce them for human consumption. Though few dare try, almost double the height of a grown man, a normally placid bison and their herd can easily tear a whole convoy to pieces.
Shellhorn is a wildly valuable leather substitute, on account of how difficult it is to procure.

*A rare but enjoyable sight is a whole swarm of [[Creatures/Lancebirds\|Creatures/Lancebirds]] descending onto a bison, only to be sent packing in a daze after bouncing off their would-be prey.
