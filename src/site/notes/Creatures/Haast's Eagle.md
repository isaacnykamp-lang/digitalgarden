*With the environment encouraging megafauna that can quickly traverse the plains, some smarter eagles have re-emerged with gigantism that can match their prey. While few in population, the Haast Eagle is a frightening bird to humans and mammals alike, and is an apex predator well-suited to life in the storm basins. 
Also known at the King Eagle to the [[Factions/Hawaiki\|Hawaiki]].
