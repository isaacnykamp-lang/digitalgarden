*A convergence of groundhog and hare, the name is owed to their freakishly sized ears that stand on end, swiveling at the slightest disturbance for danger. They stand watch over their complex warrens, able to sound out vibrations from miles away, but do them little good against ferret raiders. A favorite food of [[Creatures/Haast's Eagle\|Creatures/Haast's Eagle]].
