*One of the factors that keep the [[Factions/Disciples\|Disciples]] relevant are these iridescent, ephemeral moths that emerge from pupae upon the triggered growth of flowerbeds post-storms. Their furious pollination and breeding occurs alongside these flash events, so that the new, quickly growing caterpillars seal pollen and seeds in their cocoons and ensure that both flowers and pupated moths are ready when the next storm comes. 

*The [[Factions/Disciples\|Disciples]] of the Host collect many of these silkworms to harvest their peerless material, and the resulting fabric is the one possession upon which they name a price.
