*One might find lightning bugs, but rarer still are their multicolored counterparts. These beetles have trailing tails that they utilize in their mating display, trailing rich red lights behind them.
They can also be found in [[Dimensional Locations/The Wall\|The Wall]], as some sort of inter-dimensional transit between planes.
