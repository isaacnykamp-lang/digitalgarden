______
*A variant of the ocean-faring albatross, and a sign of the [Seeker’s Guild]. Streamsailors are a notable sign in the weather, as they often ride the winds far ahead of storms without flapping their wings for days. Their instinctive knowledge of the weather often results in a trail of travelers riding the wind alongside them. Killing one would just be inviting disaster.
Some say they're sent by the [[Gods/Moon Goddess\|Moon Goddess]] to guide travelers back into the sun.
