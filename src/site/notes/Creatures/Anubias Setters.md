_____
*This imposing breed of dog likely owes its jet-black coat to a more nocturnal bend in its past. Named after the dark jackal they resemble, they make for excellent guard dogs and fare well on long journeys. The reputed [[Anubias Caravan\|Anubias Caravan]] bears a selfsame jackal flag as their symbol.
