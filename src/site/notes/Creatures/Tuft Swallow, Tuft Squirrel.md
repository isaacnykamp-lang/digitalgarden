*Named because of their odd symbiotic relationship, squirrels and swallows build burrows together to protect from the elements. Swallows will return seeds to the squirrels, who will in return protect the nest and, in forgetting the seeds, will grow thickets of hardy shrubs to provide sanctuaries for both in [[Regions/The Diablos\|The Diablos]].
