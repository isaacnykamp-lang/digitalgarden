______
*A very common sight on the plains, large swaths of healthy pronghorns with a quick step and watchful eyes. They leave wakes of chewed up grass, and can make for an easy bite so long as travelers responsibly tend to their herds' health. They can rarely be found in stormy territory, so the paths they take make for good signs. Great food sources for [[Factions/Trappers\|Trappers]].
