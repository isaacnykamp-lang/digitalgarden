*Small colonies of porcupines travel through the desert, plugging their burrows with their sharp spines and hunting for ants and small insects. Their bright red spines can make it appear as though a coral reef is meandering through the sands, but these keratinized bands are useful for collecting moisture from the air. The color is not for show- they are venomous. Children of the [[Factions/Nomads\|Factions/Nomads]] may know the lasting pain a wound leaves, and their adults the enjoyable medicinal properties.
