*In the crags of mesas outside the [[Locations/Yavapai Desert\|Yavapai Desert]] where dust and wind are still common accompaniments, this medium sized cat makes good use of the elements. Webbed skin with cartilaginous ribs along with their impressive jump allow them to ambush unsuspecting prey, a feat of temporary flight which would appear comical on any other creature.
