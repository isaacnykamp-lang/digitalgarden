__________
The dominant language of [[Laurentia\|Laurentia]], [[Cordillera\|Cordillera]] and [[Ouachitas\|Ouachitas]]. The means of speech serves as the basic alphabet behind [[Bino\|Bino]], a non-verbal language often learnt and signaled by researchers and travelers.

General is a complicated language with a romantic Latin base, heavy on Greek rooting with influences from Eastern languages. The complication arises from its two forms, Genlong being the name of written and spoken prose. Genlong is more formal and somewhat languorous, and is thus perceived as an official or poetic verse, for specifics in documentation or better reach of implied concept.
[[Dimensional Locations/Arta\|Arta]].

Geni is the pictographic language that serves as shorthand for the larger base of Genlong and shortens major aphorisms into symbols. Its quick and evocative presentation helps the language maintain breadth as well as serves as shorthand for a more elaborate prose. Geni has a French influenced variant from the North, and a Spanish variant with a unique set of pictographs built from the selfsame alphabet (arguably easier, dominant consonants are packed into squares to read and write faster, lke hw yu knw wht I m syng dspt mst of th vwls bng drppd)

Geni in the standard variant is not immensely effective at its job due to the original Greek rooting and alphabet being heavily alliterative. The shorthand language can be easily misinterpreted, but it is argued as a strength for active listening and expression of meta-linguistics.
